\subsection*{\small{NINA Report}}
{\normalsize This is NINA's ordinary reporting to the client after completed research.\hyp{}, monitoring\hyp{} or investigative work. In addition, the series will include much of the institute's other reporting, for example from seminars and conferences, results of its own research\hyp{} and research work and literature studies. NINA Report can also be published in English, as NINA Report.
}

\subsection*{\small{NINA Theme Booklet}}
{\normalsize The booklets are prepared as needed and the series covers a wide range; from systematic definitions to information on important issues in society. The booklets usually have a popular science format with an emphasis on illustrations. NINA Thematic Booklet can also be published in English, as NINA Special Report.}

\subsection*{\small{NINA Facts}}
{\normalsize The fact sheets aim to make NINA's research results quickly and easily accessible to a wider audience. The fact sheets provide a brief presentation of some of our most important research topics.}
